ABOUT

Abegail B. Gregorio is a 3rd year college student at Isabela State University-Roxas Campus,
BSIT Major in NS. She's a committed student that adores learning new things and practising the discipline of writing poetry.
Her days are devoted to learning as she enthusiasticallyassimilate knowledge on a variety of topics. Her inquisitive nature
drives her ahead on this academic path whether she delving into the complexities of science orprobing the nuanced aspects of history.

But somewhere inside of her, buried beneath the pressures of academia, is a passionate poet.
